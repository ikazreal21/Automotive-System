About us



Our Company


South's Team is a Philippine based and licensed auto repair garage. We have been able to secure a standard and well-positioned garage facility in a central and busy district in Cavite-Philippines.


We are an auto repair services company that is set to compete in the highly competitive and fragmented auto repair and mantainance services industry not only in Cavite, but also throughout the CALABARZON market because we intend opening our outlets in key cities across the Philippines.


South's Team Auto Services, will offer services such as mechanical and electrical repair and maintenance work for cars, motorscycles, and trucks. Our business goal is to become one of the leading auto repair garages in Cavite and soon in Region-IV and we will make sure that we will give it our all to compete favorably with leaders in the industry just like other successful companies who have services centers and outlets.


Our workers selected from a pool of certifies and highly experienced auto repair engineers and technicians in the Philippines who are equipped with a good character reference and very accomodating quality that every individuals are appreciated.


We will make sure that we take all the members of our workforce throught the required trainings that will position them to meet the expectations of the company and to compete with leading auto repair garages and services centers in The United States and throughout the globe.


We will cultivate a working environment that provide a human, sustainable approach to earning a living, and living in out world, for all our partners, employess, and for our clients.


South's Team Auto Services are made by the students from Cavite State University-Carmona Campus namely, Anthony Estrera, our team leader, Delfin Maleniza, Kim Dayao, professional technician and engineers, Felix Capunihan, professional mechanical engineer, Carla Bote and Kennylyn Espiritu, professional graphic designer and concept maker.


They Have a combine experiences and professionalism to give the company's excellence, integrity, and highly recommending auto services in the Philippines.
